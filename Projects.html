On Mr. J's website, we were given the option between several different jobs within the computer science field, and we had to research information about the different occupations. The information we had to research was: a description of the job, the required education for the job, the outlook of the job, the salary ranges, if I would choose the job, and information about a university that offers a course, or a program similar to it. I was also tasked with thinking about how me taking this career path, would fulfill the vocation that God has called me to. This project was individual, and the career path I chose was computer engineering.
